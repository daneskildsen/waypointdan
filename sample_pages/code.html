Day 5: Story Bridge Climb

October 16, 2024 • Brisbane, QLD

The humidity of the Brisbane morning was already settling in by 8:00 AM as I approached the base of the Story Bridge. It's an imposing piece of steel—a cantilever bridge that feels like the industrial heart of the city. I've always preferred seeing a city from its highest points, and today was about gaining that perspective.

Strapped into a safety harness, the ascent began. The rhythmic clinking of the carabiners against the metal railing became the soundtrack of the next hour. As we reached the summit, the city unfolded. To the west, the CBD skyscrapers glinted in the sunlight; to the east, the river snaked toward Moreton Bay.

Descending was faster, the adrenaline slowly giving way to a pleasant fatigue. I spent the rest of the afternoon wandering through the Kangaroo Point Cliffs, looking back up at where I had just been. Brisbane has this understated charm—it doesn't demand your attention like Sydney, it invites you in quietly.
